The International Movement Against All Forms of Discrimination and Racism (IMADR)

A worldwide human rights organization that works to end discrimination by uniting the discriminated minorities
and improve the human rights system. IMADR's core values are empowerment, solidarity, and advocacy.
    IMADR Activities:
  • grassroots empowerment- IMADR builds grassroots movements(uses the people in a region/community for political movement) globally and connects with minority communities so they can discuss stories and support each other
  • changing perceptions- brings awareness that discrimination are issues for everyone, not just minorities. IMADR participates in events like the World Social Form that brings grassroots movements together
  • action-oriented research- research through surveys about women who faced discrimination in minority communities
E-mail: imadr@imadr.org

National Center For Transgender Equality

Social justice advocacy organization for transgender people founded in 2003 by transgender activists. Their
mission is to end discrimination and violence against transgender people thorugh education and advocacy.
    Projects:
  • Racial and Economic Justice Initiative- ensures persepctive/priorities of transgender people of color living in poverty
  • Trans Legal Services Network- ensures transgender people can go through the name and gender change process
  • Voices for Trans Equality- empowers transgender poeple to advocate for rights
  • Families for Trans Equality- network of families and groups to support families with trans youth

The Association of People Supporting Employment First

A national organization founded in 1988 that focuses on employment and career advancement
opportunities for people with disabilities.
    Goals:
  • promote employment opportunities for everyone including individuals with disabilities
  • promote and increase funding for integrated employment
  • raise awareness about the benefits of hiring individuals with disabilities
  • advocate for social change
